What We Do

We engage, enable and empower NGOs doing great work for social impact. We are currently engaged in:


Discovering small scale NGOs delivering social impact and building a free directory of such NGOs


Featuring these on our YouTube channel (free service)


Working with them on their strategy, marketing and branding (This is a paid service)


Enabling them to attract funds, including CSR funds



Why Us


NGO Champions was founded with the vision of empowering hidden gem NGOs who are delivering great social impact albeit at a small scale, and empowering them to scale.

We have been in touch with various NGOs in the past and this was one of the major issues they faced in terms of starting off with some social impact delivery but then not being sure of how to scale from there.

We intend to help such NGOs by featuring a spotlight on them, thus enabling them with a platform where they can attract the right audience and patrons for their vision.




Contact Us
